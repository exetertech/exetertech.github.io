We repair iPhones and other smartphones on request. Please contact us using the form below with the make and model of your phone as well as any damage that has been sustained. iPhone 5 screen repairs £50

Android Phones: Please email us for a custom quote

Disclaimer: We may sometimes use non OEM parts on repairs, if you would like to specifically request an OEM screen please let us know and we can see if it is possible to order one (additional costs may apply as OEM parts are more expensive). OEM Parts are the same parts used by manufacturers for refurbished phones etc, non OEM simply means 3rd party supplied parts.
