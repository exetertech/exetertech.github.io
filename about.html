About Us

We are an IT support company based in the heart of Devon, providing services for customers in Exeter and its surrounding areas. As well as providing repair services, we also run local training classes and post regular articles about the world of technology.

See our News and Advice section for more information


We have many years of technical experience, having provided IT support for businesses such as Toshiba, John Lewis and Asus. 

Our aim is to provide an effective, affordable support service for home devices and to expand throughout the Southwest.
