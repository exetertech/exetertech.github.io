Virus & Malware Removal - £40

We can help to remove Malware (malicious software) from your computer. This includes Viruses, Worms, Trojans, and generally harmful software that infects your computer system.

If you are finding a lot of popups adverts appearing, your computer is generally running slowly, or you are receiving a high amount of "spam" emails, you may have some sort of malware. We use a variety of methods to remove this and help prevent future infections.

This Service includes:

- An onsite visit from an engineer to diagnose and inspect your computer

- Removal of unwanted software or programs, possibly including a full reset of the system

- Implementation of prevention methods to help reduce the chance of similar issues in the future
