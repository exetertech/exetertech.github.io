Laptop Services

We provide many services for Laptops. See below for price guides and popular services. Please feel free to contact us if you need help with something that isn't listed.

All of our prices are flat fees. We do not charge by the hour and we do not have a "call out fee". See our terms and conditions for more details, including our full terms of service and privacy policy.

Laptop Setup - £30

We provide a service to set up your laptop. This will include an in person consultation on site to confirm your preferences for setup and includes laptops than run both Windows and Mac OS X.

This service includes:

- An onsite visit from an engineer where we will unpack and install your computer safely and securely.

- Connection to an existing broadband network.

- Reasonable device connection. We can connect your new computer to a reasonable amount of personal devices you have at your home. (For example printers and bluetooth speakers)

- Basic software setup. We will get you started with the necessary programs that you require. (For example web browsers, media/imaging applications and security software)


Estimated time of Service: 1 to 2 Hours

Virus & Malware Removal - £30

We can help to remove Malware (malicious software) from your computer. This includes Viruses, Worms, Trojans, and generally harmful software that infects your computer system.

If you are finding a lot of popups adverts appearing, your computer is generally running slowly, or you are receiving a high amount of "spam" emails, you may have some sort of malware. We can use a variety of methods to remove this and help prevent future infections.

This Service includes:

- An onsite visit from an engineer to diagnose and inspect your computer

- Removal of unwanted software or programs, possibly including a full reset of the system

- Implementation of prevention methods to help reduce the chance of similar issues in the future

Estimated time of Service: 1 to 2 Hours

Data Transfer/Backup - £30

We can transfer up to 2 terabytes of data from one device to another, or from one device to an external drive.

We will provide a portable storage device to carry the data if required.

This Service includes:

- An onsite visit from an engineer to transfer or backup the required data. We will stay to ensure the data has been successfully moved

All Customers using the data backup and recovery services must sign a disclaimer stating we will not be liable for any data loss. Every precaution will be taken to avoid the unlikely event of data loss but it is the customers responsibility to ensure there are no problems with the hard drive, computer port or connecting cables that may result in any potential issues.




Estimated time of Service: 1 Hour

Data Recovery - £50

If you are unable to access your files or have had hard drive issues, your Windows or Mac operating system may be unable to read hard drive data. In many cases we can help recover your important documents.

Depending on the severity of the issue, there are various ways to potentially save your data.

This Service Includes:

- An onsite visit from an engineer to inspect and diagnose the issue.

- Data recovery

It's important to bear in mind that this is a basic data recovery service and there are more extensive services out there, provided by forensic experts but these will be considerably more expensive.

No recovery - No fee. (£20 Inspection fee may still apply, see terms and conditions)

Broken or cracked screen? We can replace it and reinstall a new display.
